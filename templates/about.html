About Community Library

Our mission is to make books accessible to students who cannot afford them. This demo app provides a production-like skeleton that you can extend.

Our Mission

To provide free and easy access to books for students and learners from all backgrounds, ensuring education is within reach for everyone.

Our Vision

To become a community-driven library where students can borrow, learn, and grow without financial barriers, fostering a love for reading.
